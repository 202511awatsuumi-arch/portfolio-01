Create a fragrance that is uniquely yours in Asakusa

At KAORI MUSUBI, you can enjoy the full experience at a relaxed pace, from choosing fragrances to blending and taking your finished scent home. We have organized the differences between each plan and the estimated time so it is easy for first-time guests to understand.

Workshop duration: 60 to 90 minutes

How the experience flows

1

Arrive and get to know the scents

After confirming your reservation, staff will guide you through the fragrances so you can discover the atmosphere and finish that suit you best.

2

Select, layer, and refine

As you combine the scents that interest you, you will check their balance and depth while shaping them into a bottle that feels just right.

3

Confirm the finish and take it home

Once you are happy with the final impression, you can take the completed fragrance home the very same day and enjoy the memory of Asakusa afterward.

What you can enjoy during the workshop

Discover each scent's character

As you encounter each note one by one, you can find the nuance that fits your mood and your travel memories.

Enjoy the change as scents overlap

By sensing how intensity and layering change the impression, you can move closer to the finish you imagine.

Decide with confidence through guidance

Staff answer your questions and help you narrow things down, so even first-time guests can move forward without pressure.

Take the afterglow of your trip with you

After the workshop, you can enjoy your finished scent while continuing to explore Asakusa or back at your accommodation.

Workshop plans to match your itinerary

Whether you want something quick and approachable or a slower, more immersive fragrance-making session, we offer plans that are easy to choose from.
