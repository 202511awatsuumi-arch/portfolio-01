Turning the atmosphere of Asakusa into time shaped by fragrance.

KAORI MUSUBI is a fragrance-making experience inspired by the lingering feeling of Japanese culture in Asakusa.
We value the calm of modern Japanese style and the excitement of discovering something special while traveling.

What matters most at KAORI MUSUBI

  • Letting guests feel the Japanese spirit of Asakusa
  • Creating a moment that feels slightly special during a trip
  • Offering memories that continue even after the visit

Modern Japanese Style

We aim to create a refined experience that captures the atmosphere of Japanese culture in Asakusa while still feeling natural and approachable today. With soft colors and a calm atmosphere, it becomes a Japanese-inspired activity that guests can enjoy without formality.

Refined Quality

We value a calm setting and thoughtful guidance so that the fragrance-making process itself feels like a quietly luxurious part of your journey.

A Sense of Delight

We care about both the lightness that makes you think, “I want to try this,” and the satisfaction that makes you feel glad you came. We hope your finished scent gently stays with the memory of your trip.

Discover the lingering side of Asakusa that walking alone cannot reveal

Asakusa holds a quiet afterglow beneath its lively streets. KAORI MUSUBI is a workshop designed not only to observe that atmosphere, but to shape it with your own hands and take it home as part of your journey.

  • An easy duration to join even while sightseeing
  • Support that helps first-time guests relax and enjoy
  • Travel memories that continue after you leave
