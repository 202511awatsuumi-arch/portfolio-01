From arrival to take-home fragrance, everything is easy to follow.

We have outlined the experience step by step so first-time visitors can join with confidence. It also helps you picture the timing clearly while traveling.

Estimated Duration

60-90 min. varies by plan
  • Staff will guide you carefully from start to finish
  • You can take the finished item home the same day
  • Questions can be discussed in advance

Workshop Flow

1

Arrival and reception

We confirm your reservation and explain the flow of the day so you can begin the experience comfortably.

2

Try different scents

Sample Japanese-inspired and seasonal notes as you explore the atmosphere that feels right to you.

3

Discover your preference

Together with staff, sort out whether you prefer something calm, light, elegant, or expressive.

4

Blend your fragrance

Layer the scents little by little and adjust the balance until it feels like your own unique blend.

5

Check the finish

Take a moment to enjoy the completed scent and the image or feeling it leaves behind.

6

Take it home

Your finished fragrance can be taken home on the same day, letting the memory of your trip continue afterward.

Good to know before your visit

  • Please arrive about 10 minutes before your start time
  • If you are sensitive to fragrance, feel free to contact us in advance
  • We try to make the experience easy to join even when you have bags with you
  • If your travel plans may change, feel free to consult with us first
