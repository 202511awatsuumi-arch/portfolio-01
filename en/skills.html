The time spent choosing a scent becomes part of the trip itself.

The charm of this experience is not only the finished fragrance, but also discovering which scents draw you in and learning more about your own senses.
Your time in Asakusa stays in your memory together with the fragrance you create.

Three pleasures you can enjoy at KAORI MUSUBI

  • The joy of finding a scent you love
  • The joy of creating a bottle that feels like you
  • The joy of taking the afterglow of your trip home

More than photo-worthy, it is an experience that stays with you

Because fragrance is invisible, it can stay close to your senses in a gentle way. It is especially recommended if you want your memories of Asakusa to remain not only visually, but emotionally as well.

  • Enjoy it together with a friend or partner
  • Savor it slowly even on a solo trip
  • Keep the afterglow of your journey even after returning home

Recommended for guests like these

It is a great fit for travelers looking for an Asakusa-style experience, something tangible to remember their trip by, or a short activity that still feels deeply satisfying.

  • Those looking for a distinctly Asakusa experience
  • Those who want something memorable to take home
  • Those who want a satisfying experience even in a short time

Let your next moments in Asakusa stay in memory through fragrance.
